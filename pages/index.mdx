# Welcome to PCW Bug Hunters!
## Who is PCW Bug Hunters?

PCW BUG Hunters is a group of dedicated players, programmers and exploiters dedicated to finding and researching bugs inside Project CW with the purpose of making detailed bug reports directly to the development team and improving the game with their research.

## What are we doing exactly?

Everything showcased on this website is what the PCW Bug Hunters group has discovered during the first Public Alpha Playtest between 17/01/2024 and 05/02/2024 and includes our research and discoveries about bugs, technical aspects and gameplay mechanics inside the game.
